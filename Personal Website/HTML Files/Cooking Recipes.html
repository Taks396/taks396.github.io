Alfredo Pasta:


Background

At home, my family never cooked alfredo pasta. Always spaghetti, which don't get me wrong tastes great, but never alfredo pasta. That was always reserved for going out to eat at resturaunts. In college, I wanted to change that, but since I was a poor college student, I couldn't exactly go out to eat often. Hence, homemade alfredo pasta!

Alfredo Pasta: Ingredients

  • 4oz Pasta Noodles
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 2 cloves Minced Garlic or 2-3 Tbsps Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Onion Powder
  • 1/2 cups Milk
  • 1/2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream
    •
  • 1 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/4 tsp Salt or to taste
  • 1/4 tsp Pepper or to taste
    •
  • 2 Tbsps Finely Chopped Parsley

This should serve 1-2 people if I did the math right. My original recipe is the same but the numbers are slightly bigger to account for some leftovers and the existance of Bijou.

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles in a pot of water on high heat. Some sites will say boil before adding the noodles, but I find it doesn't matter as long as your checking how soft they are once it does start boiling. Drain the water once the noodles are soft to your liking.
  2. At the same time, heat your butter in a sauce pan over medium heat until it starts bubbling.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the bubbly butter and mix around until golden brown or it smells garlicy. Should be about 30 seconds.
  4. Add milk and cream into the saucepan. If you didnt add garlic earlier, add your garlic powder now. Mix until simmering.
  5. Reduce to low heat. Add the onion powder, salt, pepper, and parmesan cheese. Mix until the cheese melts and the sauce is a bit thicker than before.
  6. Remove from heat and dump drained noodles into the sauce and mix. If your saucepan isnt big enough, pour the sauce and the noodles into a different bigger bowl.
  7. Add parsely as garnish if you have it and serve.

Alfredo Pizza:


Background

So this one is funny. I made a deal with Bijou sometime near the end of college that if I could make a pizza, only then could I call myself a chef. Well that came back to bite me and I ended up makeing a really good alfredo pizza. Why alfredo? Dominos Pizza sells the best pizza in my opinion and my favorite from them is a three meat pizza with alfredo sauce instead of standard tomatoe sauce.

Alfredo Pizza: Ingredients

  • 1 ball Pizza Dough
  • 3/4 cups Alfredo Sauce
  • 8oz Low Moisture Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1.5 Tbsps Sliced Chives
  • Sliced Pepperoni
  • Olive Oil and Flour
  • Salt to taste
    •

This will produce a pizza about the same size as a Dominos medium pizza and can be cut into 4-6 slices, but only feeds 2-3 people depending on if you made a side dish.

Instructions

  1. Prepare your dough! If you're making your own, you're on your own here. If you're using store bought dough, follow the instructions on the back to let the dough rise. Mine says to keep it out of the fridge for 4 hours, then let it sit in a light sprinkling of olive oil for 8 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 500F (260C). Roll your dough around in some flour then spread it over a pizza baking tray or a normal baking tray. Keep enough dough near the edges to make a really flat bowl.
  3. Add your homemade or store bought alfredo sauce and evently spread over your dough bowl.
  4. Pour on your mozzarella cheese and spread evenly. Lightly salt the pizza.
  5. Add a layer of pepperoni slices.
  6. Move the pizza tray into the oven until the cheese is bubbling (10-15 minutes). Some ovens heat unevenly, so I rotate the pizza after about 8 minutes to try to keep the crust evenly cooked.
  7. Remove from oven and add your chives as garnish.
  8. This ones obvious, but cut it up into slices to serve each person 1-2 each. Enjoy!
